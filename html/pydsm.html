timedatetime | str

Returns

(timewindow, model_time)

pydsm.get_interpolated_cell_concentrations(tidefile: str, timewindow: str, constituent: str = 'ec') → ndarray[source]

Compute per-cell interpolated concentrations for a provided timewindow.

Produces an array shape (1, total_cells) combining upstream and downstream concentrations with linear interpolation across channel cells.
